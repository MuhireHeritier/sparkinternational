Our Work. Support Us

What we are doing to promote gender equality in Rwanda and how you can support the girls in growing their Enterprise as well as improving their standard of living.

Sewing Pads and Soap Making

The two weeks training with DfG has made the Enterprise capable of sewing Kits as well as making both liquid and bar soap. All of the products are now available for sale within Muhondo Sector and the surrounding community.

Gender Consultant Services

Spark InitiativeStaff is available to help you or your organization improve your programming surrounding gender topics and youth empowerment.

Ambassador of Women Health

As the pillar of the cooperative, the girls are certified Ambassadors of Women's Health and can train other girls and women about their health to enable the community to grow together.

Our Partners

Days for Girls

An international organization that serves to bring menstrual hygiene products to women all around the world.

They registered the enterprise, trained the girls how to sew and sell the reusable sanitary pads, and continue to provide materials and support.

Muhondo Females

All the women and girls who are currently living in Muhondo sector and may become customers of the Enterprise

They will assist with sharing information about the Enterprise and what they learn in the Health Sessions with other women and girls in their lives.

Muhondo Health Center

Health center located in Gakenke District where the beneficiaries of Spark's Gift Enterprise recieve health care on a daily basis.

The Health Center has donated space on their campus for the Enterprise Office where the girls will be able to make and sell their products.
