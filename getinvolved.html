Become a Member

Spark Initiativeis a non-profit organization that aims to support young women in Muhondo village to create an enterprise. This enterprise provide the women with ability to sew and sell reusable sanitary pads. With just an inspiration and living in this area of Muhondo village, there comes up an idea of supporting women to get affordable sanitary pads.

If you support our mission and want to get involved, we'd love to have you join our family. We're open to welcoming new volunteers and partners. Reach out to info@sparkintl.org to find out what opportunities we have available.

If you have a trip planned to Rwanda soon or you live in the area and would like to visit our Enterprise, the girls would love to have you! Reach out to info@sparkintl.org to arrange the visit.

Fundraise

If you'd like to help us raise awareness, support, and funds, we'd be incredibly grateful. We are looking for volunteers or part-time staff in this area. Please email us at info@sparkintl.org with your location, availability and resume; we'll get back to you with available opportunities.
