Who We Are.

Spark International is a non-profit organization that aims to empower young women around the world, by providing support, resources, education, and income generating opportunities. While Spark has begun its operations with a menstrual hygiene Enterprise in Muhondo, Rwanda, it will expand its work to other countries as well as programming focused on reducing the occurance of gender-based violence (GBV).

Girls and women deserve a safe space where they can seek the help and support they need after experiencing GBV, without judgement or potential triggers. Spark International seeks to create effective community-driven programming that considers local cultural norms, available resources, and community needs to reduce GBV. Spark will work with the local community to develop GBV-focused curricula and programs. These programs will include youth leadership camps; career path development; GBV awareness & prevention, self-defense; providing advocacy, support, and a safe space for survivors to recover.

How we started.

After living in Muhondo village for two years as a Peace Corps Volunteer and seeing a need for sustainable menstrual hygiene management, Aimee Carlson found a business partner, Heritier Muhire, to work with in order to bring Spark International to fruition at its first location in Muhondo in September 2018. While partnering with Days for Girls, Spark trained 10 unemployed young women and single mothers to make and sell re-usable sanitary pads as well as liquid and bar soap in their communities.

The ladies are also certified as Ambassadors of Women's Health, allowing them to train other girls and women about sexual and reproductive health, menstrual hygiene management, GBV and human-trafficking prevention, and self-defense. With just an inspiration and living in this area of Muhondo village, there comes up an idea of supporting women to get affordable sanitary pads.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

A spark is the moment right before a fire starts. Our mission is to empower women by giving them a spark, all the resources, knowledge, and space they need, regardless of their background, situation, race, sexual orientation, religion, gender identity, and ethnicity, to inspire them to take control of their futures and start a fire within themselves and their community.

Vision

Spark International's vision is to spark women into igniting their futures and becoming the brightest women possible.
